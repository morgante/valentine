Crassus Investments is a small fund, with global partners, managed in a socially-responsible way.

Social Responsibility is part of our culture

At Crassus, we believe in ethical investing by carefully considering what ventures we choose to support. For us, this means actively avoiding companies which have a negative effect on society. We also focus investments on companies which we believe have the potential to improve the human condition.

Small but growing

As a small fund, without large institutional investors, we are able to aggressively pursue opportunities which larger funds avoid due to risk or asset size. It is also a top priority to keep all our investors involved and educated about the choices we make as a fund.
